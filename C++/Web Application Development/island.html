Nyaung Oo Phee Island

Location

Nyaung Oo Pee Island is the one of 804 excellent Islands of Mergui Archipelago, in Tanintharyi Region, southern Myanmar. Nyaung Oo Phee island is situated in the Andaman Sea.It is one of the newest virgin islands of the world. The Island is secured by the virgin safari, and around of the Island is encompassed by the specific virgin white sandy shoreline (Real white and filled). Next from the shoreline into the ocean, move toward a field of the Coral. Coral reef is another whole world to explore under the water. A single place to see various kinds of beautiful colorful corals is a wonder you will remember for life.

Restaurants & Local Food

Local Cuisine: The island offers a selection of local Myanmar dishes that are rich in flavors. You’ll find a lot of fresh seafood, given the island's coastal location. Common dishes include grilled fish, prawn curries, and various seafood salads. The food is often simple but fresh and delicious, showcasing the natural ingredients from the surrounding sea.

Dining Options: Most dining options are associated with the resorts on the island. For example, the main resort on the island offers a variety of dishes, including vegetarian and vegan options, although these might be limited. The dining experience is often accompanied by beautiful beach views, making it a memorable experience for visitors

Cultural Experience: Eating on Nyaung Oo Phee Island is not just about the food, but also about experiencing the hospitality of the local Myanmar people, who are known for their friendliness and warm smiles.

Coral Reefs & Snorkeling

The Coral Reefs: TThe coral reefs around Nyaung Oo Phee Island are some of the most diverse and well-preserved in the region. These underwater ecosystems are teeming with life, showcasing a kaleidoscope of colors that range from the soft pastels of delicate coral branches to the vivid hues of various marine species. The reefs are home to a rich variety of corals, including hard corals like brain corals and staghorn corals, as well as soft corals that sway gently with the ocean currents.

Snorkeling Experience: Snorkeling at Nyaung Oo Phee Island is a must-do activity for visitors, offering an easy and accessible way to explore the island’s underwater wonders. The island’s shallow waters are perfect for both beginners and experienced snorkelers. With visibility often extending up to 30 meters, snorkelers can easily observe the vibrant coral formations and the diverse marine life that inhabits them.
